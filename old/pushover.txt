By registering Pushover devices, you can receive notifications when someone sends you a song.

How to register a pushover device:

1. Download Pushover in the App or Play Store.
2. Log in using the email 'mangonotification@gmail.com' and the password 'TaylorSwift13'.
3. Give your device a name. It can be anything, but it should contain your Mango name.
4. Enter your device name into the 'Add pushover device' field in the settings and hit enter.
5. Enter the 4-digit code you should receive via Pushover into the prompt and confirm.

You can enter multiple devices.
